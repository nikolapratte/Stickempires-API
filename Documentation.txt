Actions:
    Build(UnitType): Builds a unit of the given UnitType. Does nothing if one 
    does not have enough resources to make the unit.
Attributes (accessed with "self." notation):
    gold: Amount of gold. Updated with the update_res() method.
    mana: Amount of mana. Updated with the update_res() method.
States:
    custom: Custom match menu
    main: Main menu
    playing: In-game
    race_selection: Race selection menu
